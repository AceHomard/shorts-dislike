# Privacy Policy

**Dislike for YouTube Shorts**

Dislike for YouTube Shorts does not collect, store, or transmit any personal
data.

- The only data it keeps is a list of the Shorts you disliked, stored **locally**
  in your browser (`storage.local`) so the button shows the right state. This
  never leaves your device.
- The extension makes network requests **only to youtube.com**, as the
  logged-in user, to carry out the dislike you clicked and to read back its
  status.
- There is **no analytics, no tracking, no third-party server, and no remote
  code**.

The full source is open and public: https://github.com/AceHomard/shorts-dislike
